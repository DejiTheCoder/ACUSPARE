HEATING, VENTILATION AND AIR CONDITIONING

WHEN YOU THINK OF DESIRED AND REQUIRED TEMPERATURE FOR YOUR SPACE OR ZONE, WITHOUT CAUSING HAVOC TO THE SURROUNDING... THINK ACUSPARE

Thermal comfort and acceptable indoor air quality is one of the greatest parameters required by man and equipments surrounding him to give 100% functionality. ACUSPARE Projects & Engineering Services Limited provides best space conditioning.

HEATING: Water/Steam through pipes and heat exchangers. Also, warm air distribution through duct-work systems of supply and return air through metal or fibreglass ducts.

VENTILATION: Natural and Mechanical / Forced ventilation systems.

AIR CONDITIONING: High efficiency free cooling, central systems and dehumidification. We design and install our HVAC equipments following regulations and standards of organisations such as HARDI, ASHRAE, SMACNA, ACCA, Uniform Mechanical Code, International Mechanial Code, and AMCA.
